OPSC PGT Recruitment 2024 Eligibility criteria

Educational qualification

Candidates should have completed Master Degree in Concerned Subject with B.Ed from a recognized University.

B.Ed Qualifications for subjects other than :

Botany, Chemistry, English, Economics, Geography, History, Hindi, Mathematics, Odia, Physics, Political Science, Sanskrit and Zoology shall not be mandatory eligibility criteria for post of Post Graduate Teacher.
