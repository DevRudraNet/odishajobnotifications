Selection process

The selection process will be completely based on these stages which is mentioned below. Candidates selected by the process of

  1. Written Examination
  2. Physical Measurement Test (PMT)
  3. Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  4. Document Verification

1. OSAP IRB Physical Measurement Test (PMT)

For UR/SEBC (Men)

  • Height - 168cm
  • Weight - 55 kg
  • Chest - unexpanded - 79cm, expand - 84cm

For SC/ST (Men)

  • Height - 163cm
  • Weight - 50 kg
  • Chest - unexpanded - 76cm, expand - 81cm

2. OSAP IRB Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

  • Running - 1.6 kms in 6 minutes.
  • Broad Jump - 4.00 meters in 3 attempts
  • High Jump - 1.38 meters in 3 attempts
