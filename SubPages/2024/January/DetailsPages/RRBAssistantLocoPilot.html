Salary

19,900 - 56,600 per month.

Selection process

The selection process will be completely based on these stages which is mentioned below. Candidates selected by the process of

  • CBT - I
    • The 1st stage of CBT is based on 75 questions.
    • Each question carries 1 mark, making the total marks 75.
    • Questions are from Mathematics, General Science, General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Awareness, and Current Affairs.
    • Sections and marks distribution:
      • Mathematics - 20 questions and 20 marks
      • General Intelligence and Reasoning - 25 questions and 25 marks
      • General Science - 20 questions and 20 marks
      • General Awareness on Current Affairs - 10 questions and 10 marks
    • Total of 75 questions and 75 marks.
    • 60 minutes Time Duration.
    • Negative Marking: ¹/³ rd for each incorrect answer.
  • CBT - II
    • The 2nd stage of CBT is based on two parts - Part A and Part B.
    • Part A and B together have 175 questions.
    • Each question carries 1 mark, making the total marks 175.
    • Part A questions are from Mathematics, Basic Science, Engineering, General Intelligence and Reasoning, and General Awareness.
    • Part B questions consist of relevant trade.
    • Sections and marks distribution:
      • Mathematics
      • General Intelligence and Reasoning
      • General Science
      • General Awareness
    • Total of 100 marks for Part A and 75 marks for relevant trade in Part B.
    • Grand Total - 175 marks.
  • CBAT - Computer Based Aptitude Test
    • Candidates must score a minimum of 42% marks in each test to qualify.
    • Candidates are shortlisted based on a 70% score in Part A.
