RPF Recruitment 2024 Eligibility criteria

Educational qualification
For Sub-inspector -

Candidates should have completed Bachelor Degree from a recognized University.

For Constable -

Candidates should have completed at least 10th class (SSLC or it's equivalent) from a recognized Board.

Vacancy Details :

  1. Constable - 2000 Posts
  2. Sub-inspector - 250 Posts

  • Minimum Age - 18 Years

  • Age Relaxation - As per government Rules.
  • For OBC Students - 03 Years

Salary Information

As per OPSC good salary is paid.

Selection Process

  1. CBT
  2. Pshycial Efficiency Test
  3. Pshycial Measurement Test
  4. Document Verification

CBT Exam Pattern -

  1. General Awareness - 50 Questions and 50 Marks
  2. Arithmetic - 35 Questions and 35 Marks
  3. General Intelligence and Reasoning - 35 Questions and 35 Marks

Total 120 Marks 90 Minutes Time Duration.

RPF PET for Constable -

  1. Physical Efficiency Test (PET) for Constable:
    1. For Male:
      • 1600 meters in 05 minutes 45 seconds
      • Long Jump: 14 feet
      • High Jump: 4 feet
    2. For Female:
      • 800 meters in 03 minutes 40 seconds
      • Long Jump: 09 feet
      • High Jump: 03 feet
  2. Physical Efficiency Test (PET) for SI:
    1. For Male:
      • 1600 meters in 06 minutes 30 seconds
      • Long Jump: 12 feet
      • High Jump: 3 feet 9 inches
    2. For Female:
      • 800 meters in 04 minutes
      • Long Jump: 09 feet
      • High Jump: 03 feet
  3. Physical Standard Test (PST) for Constable and SI:
    1. For UR/OBC:
      • Height in cms:
        • Male: 165
        • Female: 157
      • Chest in cms (only for Male): 80 - 85
    2. For SC/ST:
      • Height in cms:
        • Male: 160
        • Female: 152
      • Chest in cms (only for Male): 76.2 - 81.2
    3. For Gorahwalis/Gorkhas/Marathas/Dogras/Kumaonese and other categories specified by Govt:
      • Height in cms:
        • Male: 163
        • Female: 155
      • Chest in cms (only for Male): 80 - 85
