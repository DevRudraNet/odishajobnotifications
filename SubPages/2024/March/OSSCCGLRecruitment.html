OSSC CGL Recruitment 2024 ! Apply Online For GST Officer, Auditor And Inspector Posts

Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC) recently announced a notification for Combined Graduate Level Recruitment Examination (CGLRE) 2024. So, in this article we have covered all the essential information about OSSC CGL Recruitment 2024, including Eligibility Criteria, Application Process, Important Date and all the required notifications about OSSC CGL Recruitment 2024.

OSSC CGL Recruitment 2024 Details

Organization name

- Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC)

Category

- Odisha government job

Type of job

-Permanent

Name of the Post

-Inspector of Endowments, GST Officer, Auditor etc

Application mode

-Online

Place of posting

- Odisha

Who can apply -

- All Odisha Candidates

Last Date

- 05/04/2024

Gender

- Both Male and Female

OSSC CGL Recruitment 2024 Eligibility criteria

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed Bachelor's Degree Examination with computer knowledge education conducted by any recognised University.

OSSC CGL Vacancy List 2024

Total Vacancies - 595 (W-195)

  1. Inspector of Endowments (Group-B) - 21 (W-07) posts
  2. Assistant CT & GST Officer (Group-B) - 61 (W-20) posts
  3. Auditor (Group-B) - 09 (W-03) posts
  4. Inspector of Cooperative Societies (Group-B) - 15 (W-05) posts
  5. Junior Assistant (Group-C) - 478 (W-156) posts

Age limit

As per OSSC CGL Recruitment Official Notification 2024 notifications candidates age limit is on 01/01/2024

  • Minimum Age - 21 Years
  • Maximum Age - 38 Years
  • Age Relaxation - As per government Rules.
  • For OBC Students - 03 Years
  • For ST/SC/Pwd - 05 Years
  • For Ex-Service man - 05 years
  • For PWD+SC/ST- 15 Years
  • For more Information please visit its official website.

SOSSC CGL Salary

For Group-B post - Rs.35,400 - Rs.1,12,400 (Level-9)

For Group-C post - Rs.19,900 - Rs.63,200 (Level-4)

Selection process

The selection process will be consist in the different stages to confirm the recruitment eligible and qualified candidates. The selection stages include -

  1. Preliminary and Written Examination
  2. Physical Standard Measurement Test
  3. Document Verification

Application Fees

As per OSSC CGL Recruitment notification 2024 candidates have not to pay an application fees for complete the online application process.

  1. For General Candidates:free
  2. For EWS Candidates: free
  3. For OBC Candidates:free
  4. For phd Candidates : free
  5. For SC/ST Candidates: free
  6. For more information please visit its official website.
