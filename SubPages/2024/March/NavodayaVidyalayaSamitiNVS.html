Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti NVS Recruitment 2024 Eligibility criteria

Education Qualifications:
  1. Stenographer:
    • 12th pass from a recognized University
    • Shorthand writing - 10 minutes @ 80 w.p.m.
    • Transcription - 50 minutes (English) / 65 minutes (Hindi) on computer.
  2. Junior Secretarial Assistant:
    • 12th pass from a recognized Board/University/Institution
    • Computer typing - 30 w.p.m in English / 25 w.p.m in Hindi
  3. Computer Operator:
    • BCA/B.Sc./BE/B.Tech (Computer Science/IT) from a recognized University.
  4. Catering Supervisor:
    • Bachelor's Degree in Hotel Management from an Institution recognized by Ministry of Tourism Govt. of India/State Government.
  5. Electrician Cum Plumber:
    • 10th class pass
    • ITI Certificate in the trade of Electrician/Wireman
    • At least 2 years experience in electrical Installation/wiring/plumbing
  6. Lab Attendant:
    • 12th class with Science stream from a recognized Board/University OR 10th class with a certificate/Diploma in Laboratory Technique.
  7. Mess Helper:
    • 10th class pass
    • 05 years experience of working in a Govt. residential organization's Mess/School's Mess
  8. Multi Tasking Post:
    • Candidates must pass minimum Matriculation (10th) from a recognized board.
