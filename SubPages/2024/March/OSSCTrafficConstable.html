Selection process

The selection process will be consist in the different stages to confirm the recruitment eligible and qualified candidates. The selection stages include -

  1. Preliminary and Written Examination
  2. Physical Standard Measurement Test
  3. Document Verification

Exam Pattern -

Candidates needs to appear for a CBT test that acess their knowledge in following subjects -

  1. General Awareness - 50 Questions and 50 Marks
  2. Arithmetic - 35 Questions and 35 Marks
  3. General Intelligence and Reasoning - 35 Questions and 35 Marks

Total 120 Marks

90 Minutes Time Duration.
