Written Examination details
  1. For Engineering Category:
    • Theory (60) & Practical (40) of Craftsman Training Scheme curriculum in relevant trade (Total-100 marks).
    • Workshop Calculation and science (15), Engineering Drawing (15), Employability Skills (10), and English (10) (Total 50 marks).
  2. For Non-Engineering Category:
    • Theory (60) & Practical (40) of Craftsman Training Scheme curriculum in relevant trade (Total-100 marks).
    • Employability Skill subject (40) and English (10) (Total 50 marks).
  3. For Diploma/Degree Category:
    • Diploma level 1st year common syllabus (90 marks) and Entrepreneurship, Management & Smart Technology (10 marks) (Total-100 marks).
    • Diploma level Engineering Drawing (20), Engineering Mathematics (20), and English (10) (Total 50 marks).

20 marks shall be awarded by the Commission after the Written Examination to the candidates who have possessed Craft Instructors Training Scheme (CITS) examination final pass certificate under the of NCW in the relevant trade.
