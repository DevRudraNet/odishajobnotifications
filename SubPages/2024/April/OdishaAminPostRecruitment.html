Odisha Amin Post Recruitment 2024 Eligibility criteria

Education Qualifications:

Eligibility Criteria

  • Caretaker: Candidates must have completed 10+2 Level.
  • Ayurvedic Assistant: Candidates must have completed +2 science or equivalent exam from a recognised Board/Council/University.
  • Unani Assistant: Candidates must have completed +2 science or equivalent exam from a recognised Board/Council/University.
  • Junior Fisheries Technical Assistant:
    • Candidates must have completed +2 Vocational (Fisheries) from CHSE and must know swimming well.
    • Candidates possessing +2 science qualification will also be eligible to apply for the post.
    • If candidates possessing +2 Vocational (Fisheries) will not be available, then candidates with +2 science qualification will be considered for selection.
  • Amin: Candidates must have completed +2 Arts/Science/Commerce or such other qualification as are equivalent to +2 examination with certificate on basic computer skills from a recognised University.
