Odisha Agriculture Department Recruitment Eligibility criteria

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed

  1. For YP - M.Sc Biotechnology/ Biotechnology / Botany from a recognised University.
  2. For Project Associate - M.Sc Agriculture / Biotechnology/ Botany from a recognised University.
  3. For Field Worker - B.Sc Honours in Botany / Zoology / Biotechnology from a recognised University.

Age Criteria

  1. Minimum Age: 21 Years
  2. Maximum Age:
    • 35 Years (for general)
    • 40 Years (for OBC)
    • 50 Years (for SC/ST)

Age Relaxation

Age relaxation is applicable as per government rules:

  • For OBC Students: 3 Years
  • For ST/SC/PWD: 5 Years
  • For Ex-Serviceman: 5 Years
  • For PWD + SC/ST: 15 Years

For more information, please visit the official website.

Salary Information

  1. For YP - ₹35,000 per month
  2. For Field Worker - ₹18,000 per month
  3. For Project Associate - ₹25,000 per month

Application Fees Information

  • For General Candidates: Free
  • For EWS Candidates: Free
  • For OBC Candidates: Free
  • For PhD Candidates: Free
  • For SC/ST Candidates: No Fees for All

For more information, please visit the official website.
