CISF ASI Requirment 2023 Eligibility criteria

CISF ASI Educational qualification

To be eligible for CISF ASI DLCE Recruitment candidates must have completed certain qualification which is set as per CISF .

Candidates should have completed +3 (Bachelor's Degree) in any stream (Arts/Science/Commerce), from a recognised University.

The candidates should have completed 05 years of regular service including basic training in the grade or 05 years combined regular service as Head Constable/ GD Constable / GD and Constable/ TM as on 01.08.2023.
